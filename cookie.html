Cookies Policy

At Quest Senior Solutions, we are committed to ensuring the privacy and security of your personal information. Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience and provide personalized Services.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

Types of Cookies

We utilize the following types of cookies:

  • Functional cookies: These cookies enable basic functionality of the website.
  • Analytical cookies: These cookies help us analyze and improve the performance of our website.
  • Marketing cookies: These cookies are used to personalize the advertising content that you see.

Cookie Usage

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website, understand how you use our website, and provide relevant content.

Cookie Preferences and Management

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Please refer to your browser's help documentation for information on how to block or delete cookies.

Data Privacy and Security

We prioritize the security and privacy of your personal information. Our use of cookies complies with data protection laws, and we are committed to protecting your data.
